Hey dear user, I'm Joana

Let me introduce myself, I'm from the city that doesn't exist (Leiria), or so they say.

I have the curiosity and passion of a child, mixed with the logical thinking of a chess player. My favourite part of being a Product Designer? Turning spider webs into simple flows.

I'd say I'm extroverted, but I think about a thousand things at once, sometimes even I struggle to keep up. I keep reminding myself to take it easy.

If you're looking for a designer who talks to users before touching Figma, connects dots nobody noticed, and somehow still ships on time, hi, that's me. Fair warning: I will ask why. A lot.

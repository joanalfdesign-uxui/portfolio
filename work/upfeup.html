The challenge

A process built on emails and spreadsheets

FEUP students applying for standalone course units had to navigate a fragmented process — submitting forms manually, waiting for email responses, and having no visibility on the status of their application.

The goal was to design a platform that replaced this entirely: structured application submission, an admin review workflow, and a clear tracking experience for students from day one to final result.
